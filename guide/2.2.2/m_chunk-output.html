m:chunk-output

m:chunk-output — Performs the final-pass of processing on chunks.

Mode:
{http://docbook.org/ns/docbook/modes}chunk-output
Defined in:
modules/chunk-output.xsl (7)

Description

Chunks (see Section 2.7, ““Chunked” output”) are constructed in several passes. The last pass is in the m:chunk-output mode. This mode makes last minute adjustments and produces the individual chunk documents.
