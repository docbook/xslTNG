$verbatim-embellishments — Attempt to embellish verbatim environments?

Parameter:
{}verbatim-embellishments
Defined in:
param.xsl
Used in:
main.xsl, param.xsl, modules/chunk-cleanup.xsl
Since:
2.5.0

Synopsis

$verbatim-embellishments := 'true'

Description

When formatting verbatim environments, this flag controls whether or not any attempt will be done to number lines, add syntax highlighting, or identify callouts. Enabling this flag doesn’t necessarily mean that the embellishments will succeed (that depends on other settings; see Section 2.11.2, “Syntax highlighter”).

If this flag is true, embellishments may be attempted. If it is not, the listings will always be presented without embellishment.
