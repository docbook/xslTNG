m:generate-titlepage

m:generate-titlepage — Generate a title page.

Mode:
{http://docbook.org/ns/docbook/modes}generate-titlepage
Defined in:
modules/templates.xsl

Description

All “block” elements that have a title (or another feature that constitutes a title) are processed in the m:generate-titlepage mode to generate their “title page”. Don’t be mislead by the term “title page”. For many elements, the title page consists of nothing more than a single header element.
