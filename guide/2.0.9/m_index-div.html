m:index-div

m:index-div — Mode for generating divisions in an index.

Mode:
{http://docbook.org/ns/docbook/modes}index-div
Defined in:
modules/index.xsl

Description

Index processing, that is, the process of generating an index, involves collecting all of the indexterms together, collating them, and rendering them.

The terms are grouped alphabetically and each group is processed in the m:index-div mode.
