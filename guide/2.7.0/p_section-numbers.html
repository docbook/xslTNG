$section-numbers — Are sections numbered?

Parameter:
{}section-numbers
Defined in:
param.xsl
Used in:
main.xsl, param.xsl

Synopsis

$section-numbers as xs:string := 'true'

Description

If $section-numbers is true, sections that do not have an explicit label will be labeled with their section number.

See Section 3.6, “Controlling numeration”.
