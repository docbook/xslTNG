$refentry-generate-name — Use “Name” as the title of a refentry .

Parameter:
{}refentry-generate-name
Defined in:
param.xsl
Used in:
main.xsl, param.xsl

Synopsis

$refentry-generate-name := true()

Description

If $refentry-generate-name is true, the title of the refentry page will be the word “Name”. Otherwise its value depends on the setting of $refentry-generate-title. Generally, exactly one of $refentry-generate-name or $refentry-generate-title should be true.
