m:crossref-number-separator — Produce the cross-reference number separator.

Mode:
{http://docbook.org/ns/docbook/modes}crossref-number-separator
Defined in:
modules/xref.xsl

Description

The m:crossref-number-separator mode is used to generate the separator between a number and what follows it in a cross-reference.
